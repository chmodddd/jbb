<p>
I have grouped the patterns by type, as they were in old-fashioned knitting shops;  some patterns may fit into more than one category, and the beauty of the virtual world is that a pattern can happily live in more than one drawer!   There is also a "retro" category which is entirely arbitrary, and roughly includes anything prior to the 1980s.  If you are looking for something very particular or obscure, then try a specific search.</p>
<p>
I'm not attempting to run a business, as such, but I will endeavour to offer a professional enough service on an ad hoc basis.<br> I hope you will find something you like. </p>
